Primary care can be provided by pediatricians, family medicine and internal medicine physicians, along with nurse practitioners and physician's assistants.

How often should i see my primary care doctor?

How often you should see your primary care doctor depends on things like your age, health, and any medical conditions you may have. Your doctor will help you figure out the best schedule for you, whether that's a yearly check-up or more frequent visits to stay on top of your health. Regular visits help keep you healthy and can catch problems early. Know when to schedule important regular exams.

What should i bring to my primary care visit?
